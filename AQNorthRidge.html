Cashier Messages

Keep your workspace clean and organized!! It is the first thing people notice if you have a messy workstation. Represent the City to the best of your abilities and start by having a great looking place to work at! Report cards and other important documents will not become misplaced if they are stored properly away, while also being easier to find when needed quickly.

E-mail is one of our primary methods of communication. You should be opening outlook and reading e-mails each shift. Please ensure you read all of the messages between when you were here last and now. Reply to messages as requested. MOST CASHIERS SEEMS TO BE MISSING IMPORTANT NOTICE. It is also important that you review the calendar in outlook, as well as facility booking for the day before each shift. If you need help with this, please ask.
