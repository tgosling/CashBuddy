Act-i-Pass Info

What is this program?

The ACT-I-pass program is an initiative that aims to promote physical activity for Grade 5 students within the Middlesex-London area. This is accomplished by providing FREE access to recreational programs with their partners.

How do they Join?

Students receive a hardcopy of the registration package through their school prior to starting their Grade 5 year or they can apply online on the In-Motion webpage.

How does this impact us?

City of London Aquatic Services is one of the ACT-I-Pass partners. This partnership entitles the ACT-I-Pass cardholder to free admission to any of our recreational swims throughout the year. In addition to the cardholder's admission, this pass also grants an additional +1 admission.(Typically a friend or parent)

What are we required to do?

After completing the registration process the client should receive this card.


After showing any City of London pool this card, complete a pass registration for the client. There is an option under plan type for ACT-I-Pass. They should also be issued a key tag ID.

After receiving this key tag the ACTI-Pass owner can use this key tag for their own admission as well as a +1. This +1 can be used for either a parent or friend, and it is rung in as a complimentary pass in Point of Sale. Please note that normal admission policy and all other requirements are still in effect.
