Wading Pools

Wading pools are a Free oppurtunity for young children to gain their confidence in the water.

Wading pools have a maximum depth of 0.6m (2') and have a lifeguard on site at all times

Cloth and disposable diapers are not permitted. We recommend swim diapers.
