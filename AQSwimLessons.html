Lifesaving Society Swim for Life

We are pleased to offer the Lifesaving Society's Swim for Life program as our learn to swim program facilities

Parent and Tot: 3 level program for children 4 months to 3 years of age.

  • Because activities and progressions are based on child development, you register in the level appropriate for your child's age: 4-12 months, 12-24 months, or 2-3 years.

Preschool: 5-level program for children 3 to 5 years of age

  • Give your child a head start on learning to swim. The Lifesaving Society Preschool Program develops an appreciation and healthy respect for the water before these kids get in too deep.
  • In our basic aquatic progressions, we work to ensure 3 to 5-year olds become comfortable in the water and have fun acquiring and developing a foundation of water skills. We incorporate Lifesaving Society Water Smart education in all Preschool levels.

Swimmer: 9 level program for children 5+ years of age

  • The Lifesaving Society's 6-level Swimmer program makes sure your children learn how to swim before they get in too deep.
  • Swimmer progressions accommodate children 5 years and older including absolute beginners as well as swimmers who want to build on the basics. We stress lots of in-water practice to develop solid swimming strokes and skills.
  • We incorporate Lifesaving Society Water Smart education in all Swimmer levels.
