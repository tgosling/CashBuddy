Cashier Messages

FUN FACT
The phone at the front desk rings both at the cashier desk and deck supervisor office. So if you don't pick up the phone upstairs, it rings at both phones until picked up.

PLEASE DO NOT CHANGE THE VOLUME ON THE PHONE, THE FACILITY GETS QUITE LOUD WHEN BUSY AND IT IS IMPORTANT TO PICK UP ALL CALLS. IF IT IS RINGING TOO LOUD, PICK UP THE PHONE QUICKLY AND THE NOISE WILL STOP.

E-mail is one of our primary methods of communication. You should be opening outlook and reading e-mails each shift. Please ensure you read all of the messages between when you were here last and now. Reply to messages as requested. MOST CASHIERS SEEMS TO BE MISSING IMPORTANT NOTICES. It is also important that you review the calendar in outlook, as well as facility booking for the day before each shift. If you need help with this, please ask.

Report Card Pick-up

For swimmers who missed the last day or left their report cards behind, they can be found in the Pick Up - File by Last Name drawer located just left of the computer towers at the CGAC front desk.

C.G.A.C. Facility Facts

Facility Features
  • 50 metre 8 lane pool
  • Shallow-End Depth: 24"(2 feet)-48"(4 feet)
  • Middle-to-Deep Depth: 48"(4 feet)-13'5"
  • Diving springboards: One 1m, Two 3m
  • Diving platforms: 3m, 5m, 7m, 10m
  • Main Tank Capactiy: 495,000 US Gallons
  • Total Capacity(Bather load): 495
  • Heated Whirlpool
  • 3 Universal Changerooms
  • 1 Handicap Accessible Changeroom
Tempertatures

  • Whirlpool: 37-42*celsius
