Techinical Issues Resolution Guide Sheet

This document is intended to provide you with guidance on how to reach IT issues in your business area - especially during off hours. During regular business hours (Monday through Friday 8:00am-8:00pm) log an ITS Service Incident(Chrome or IE supported) or call TSD @ ext. 5712. DO NOT EMAIL SPECIFIC STAFF AS THERE IS NO GUARANTEE THAT THEY ARE AVAILABLE

*Green:

This is an issue that requires action on the part of ITS but has no immediate timeline.
Examples:
- Movement of equipment
- Additional PC's, telephones, etc.
Action: Log an ITS Service Incident. STAFF should be aware that these will not be addressed until the next business day.

*Yellow:

Issues that cause some inconvenience to staff but are generally not impacting levels of cutomer service.
Examples
-Receipt printer not working in a building where other equipment that is functioning is available
- Cash drawer will not open but key is available.
-PC is offline or specific issues with one PC where others are available
Action: Log an ITS Service Incident. STAFF should be aware that these will not be addressed until the next business day.

*Red:

Issue is business critical and immediate action is required. Issue has significant impact on ability of staff to conduct business and has a negative impact on custmer service
Examples
- All PC's are down or not responding
- Telephones are not working
- All debit PIN pads are not working and cannot be re-initialized at the location
