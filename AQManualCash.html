POS Troubleshooting Guide

When POS Stops Responding

Step 1

Notify your Deck Supervisor and begin filling out Manual Cash Tracking sheet. Leave your drawer slightly ajar so you can put money in it. On the tracking sheet make a note of how many participants come to child, adult and senior rec swim, fit lanes etc. For pass holders record 6-digit barcode on front of their card.

Step 2

At first opportunity restart computer. Fill out POS Incident form. If POS does not respond for the remainder of the shift please see below.

Step 3

When cashing out at the end of the shift record the totals of participants and dollar value at the bottom of each column. Count your float and money as usual and fill out envelope. Attach deposit flap to your Manual Cash Tracking Form and your drawer balancing report for any transactions previously entered. Please record on your Manual Cash Tracking sheet that transactions were NOT ENTERED.

If POS Begins Working At Any Time

Step 1

Take Manual Cash Tracking Form and enter admissions and passes.

Step 2

When cashing out attach Manual Cash Tracking Form to report and flap. Please ensure that you have entered the items and noted this on top of Manual Cash Tracking Form.

The Manual Cash Tracking form can be found here
