Subsidy

The City of London's Play You Way Fund offers financial support to low income Londoners who want to access recreation programs and activities offered by the City of London. Once approved, funding can be accessed for the next 12 months, up to a maximum of $300 per person. Program credits are not transferable between family members or between years. The Play Your Way Fund does not apply to facility rentals or birthday parties.

To be eligible for Play Your Way Program, applicants must be:

  • 18 years of age or older
  • City of London resident
  • have a amily income that falls within the ranges below, based on Statistic's Canada Low Income Cut-Off(LICO)

Here's how to apply:
Bring or email your completed application form with copies of supporting to one of the locations listed at the end of this document.

1. For identification purposes, please provide any One of the following documents for each family member:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Canadian Citizenship, permanent resident card or Refugee documents
  • Certiicate of Indian Status
  • Canadian Passport
  • Valid Ontario Driver's License
  • Ontario Photo ID card

2. The following is a list of applicable recods of income. Please attach a current copy of One of these sources:

  • Canada Revenue Agency notice of assessment from current year for Each Adult in the household, showing line #236
  • Canada Revenue Agency Goods & Services Tax/Harmonized Sales Tax stating family net income
  • Canada Child Tax Benefit summary stating familys Adjusted Annual Income
  • Government benefits stub: OW, ODSP/ACSB, EIB, CCP, OAS, GAINS, etc or direct deposit statement

Accepting Subsidy for Admissions

All cashiers are able to apply a customer's Parks and Recreation subsidy to a transaction such as a recreational swim, lane swim, or aquafit admission. Previously, subsidy could only be used for registrations and memberships.

Subsidy is like our memberships. People may NOT share subsidy. The only time where you may use one person's subsidy for multiple people is if a parent wants to purchase a family admission. Otherwise, you must repeat this process for each person.

Step 1

Open Point of Sale. Click on Client Search
Your screen may also have a subsidy button. If so, you may use that instead of client search

Step 2

A "Scan/Find Client" box will come up:

a) Scan the customer's keytag or type their name or phone number.
b) If multiple clients are found, select the appropriate client from the list

Step 3

Click the admission type that you wish to sell, i.e. REC SWIM ADULT

c) A "Enter Subsidy Amount" box will come up. Customers have a maximum of $300 per year that can be used for subsidy. If they have more available subsidy, then the max subsidy will be the admission cost. You may subsidize all or part of the admission cost. For example, if the customers wishes to pay $2.00 out of pocket for a $5.00 admission, you would enter $3.00 in the apply field and click ok. If the customers wants to use subsidy for the full cost of the admission, enter the maximum amound and click ok.

Step 4

To complete the transaction, click "Balance on Account"
The transaction window will say "Completed"
