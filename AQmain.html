Cash Buddy

Welcome to Cash Buddy. This is designed to help you learn all the information necessary to do your job adequately. Cashiers frequently get asked question from the general public about all provided aquatic services. Cash Buddy will try to give you all the information you need to know in order to answer and help all patrons. Any Questions, Comments or Concerns can be sent to Tyler Gosling on Staff Center
Any Input is greatly appreciated, this has been made to make your job easier and make you a more efficient employee for the public. ANY AND ALL COMMENTS TO MAKE THE SITE BETTER WILL BE CONSIDERED.

Admission Policy

All Children 12 & Under

No Swim Test or Unsuccesful Swim Test

All children 12 & under will receive an orange wristband upon entry and must be directly supervised in the water at all times by a responsible person 13 years of age or older. The supervising person must be able to touch the pool bottom

Successful Swim Test

Children may take a swim test consisting of a 20m swim (or the width of the pool if further) without touching the bottom. If successful, swimmers will receive a green wristband and must be supervised in the facility

Responsible Persons 13-17

Must identify as a "swimmer" or "non-swimmer" upon entry. Swimmers will be given a green wristband permitting full access to the pool.

Swim Test Admission Cards

The Swim Test Admission Card is available to swimmers who have succesfully completed a swim test. These individuals will be able to be issued a green wristband when entering the pool
Cashiers may give out cards ONLY if the swimmer has a green wristband securely fastened on their arm

Refund Policy

We do not issue direct refunds for admissions. If a customer requests a refund, please direct them to the PM/APM. They will be able to offer a complimentary pass if necessary.

If the City of London cancesl a program, a full refund will be issued.

No refunds can be issued after the second class, except in extenuating circumstances.

Administrative fees may be added depending on the situation. Please consult Page 3 in the Spectrum, or call a registration clerk.

Important Dates

Expectations of Front Desk Staff

  • Be completely knowledgeable of the information in your job description, the cashier manual, and the policy manual. You are often the go-to person when a customer has a question.
  • Only individuals who are cash trained at the summer trainings can assume responsibility for handling money.
  • A staff member may not leave cash or related materials in an unsecured area.
  • When at the pool, your immediate supervisors are the Pool Manager (PM) and the Assistant Pool Manager (APM). Any questions/problems regarding the pool should be directed to him/her.
  • Your actions are a reflection on the Corporation. You should conduct yourself in a professional and mature manner at all times when on duty and on site at the facility.
  • Cashiers are not expected to attend the in-service trainings at the pools.
  • Any disregard of the procedures or rules in this manual or the Aquatics Policy Manual is subject to disciplinary actions.
