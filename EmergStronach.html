IN AN EMERGENCY

  1. Take a deep breath and stay calm.
  2. Dial 9 Then 911
  3. The dispatcher will say "ambulance, fire, police"
    State the service(s) you require. (i.e. ambulance)
  4. When asked, our address and phone number are:
    1221 Sandford Street
    London ON N5V 2J8
    (519) 661-4426
  5. The Dispatcher may ask you to describe the emergency, the condition of the victim, or how the situation is being treated (i.e. staff may be oerforming CPR). Answer to the best of your knowledge
  6. Hang up Last
  7. Go to the staff dealing with the emergency and realy that the 911 call has been made.
  8. Send a staff member to meet the emergency personnel.
