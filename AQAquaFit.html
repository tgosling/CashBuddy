Aquafit Info

Aqua fit is a great exercise program for patrons rehabilitating injuries or looking to add more physical activity into their daily lives. None of the classes are meant to be initimidating with their class names, and are intended for patrons to go at their own pace. We would rather have them exercising in the water and enjoying the facilities, than shying away from the program all together.
